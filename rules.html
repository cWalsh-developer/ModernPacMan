PAC-BLOKE RULES

Everything a first-time player needs before starting a game.

Goal

  • Clear all dots and power pellets to complete the level.
  • Avoid ghosts unless they are scared (blue).
  • Survive with your lives and chase the highest score.

Controls

  • Arrow Keys: Move Pac-Bloke
  • Space: Activate Split Power (when available and in power mode)
  • Shift: Use Teleport (when available)
  • P: Pause
  • R: Restart
  • M: Mute/Unmute

Scoring

  • Dot: 10 points
  • Power Pellet: 50 points
  • Eat a Ghost while scared: 200 points
  • Fruit gives bonus points and may grant power uses.

Power Mode

  • Power pellets usually trigger Power Mode.
  • In Power Mode, ghosts become scared and can be eaten for points.
  • Scared state is temporary, so act quickly.

Level 5+ Inverse Mode

  • From Level 5 onward, eating a power pellet can trigger Inverse Mode instead of normal power mode.
  • In Inverse Mode, arrow-key controls are inverted.
  • Arrow Up moves down, and Arrow Down moves up.
  • Arrow Right moves left, and Arrow Left moves right.
  • You control the ghost-like player while all four ghosts chase you as Pac-Men.
  • Inverse Mode is temporary, then normal behavior returns.

Special Powers

  • Split Power (Space): Spawns split Pac-Blokes to hunt ghosts during power mode.
  • Teleport (Shift): Teleports you to a random valid location.
  • Both powers have limited charges. Watch the status display.

Ghost Behavior

  • Ghosts chase continuously with slightly different targeting styles.
  • They leave the ghost house in quick sequence at the start of each level.
  • If you lose a life, avoid risky corners immediately after respawn.

Quick Tips

  • Use early turn buffering: press your turn slightly before intersections.
  • Save teleport for panic moments or dead-end escapes.
  • Clear edges and tunnels smartly to avoid being trapped.
